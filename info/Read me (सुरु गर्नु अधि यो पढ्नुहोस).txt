Introducing OSEL, a free, user-friendly grade sheet and ledger app for schools. This app is designed to make the process of managing student grades and other data simple and efficient. With multiple features such as EMIS import, teachers and administrators can easily import student data from their school's EMIS system. The app also allows for easy data entry, tracking, and reporting of grades, attendance, and more. Whether you're a teacher, administrator, or student, OSEL is the perfect tool for managing student data. Try it out today and see the difference it can make in your school. It's totally free software made for nepali school.
